<h2 id="description">Description</h2>
		<p>«SITE-NAME» is an archive of «NAME»'s personal projects - notably «NAME»'s puzzles - with a triple purpose: record «NAME»'s learning journey, make it useful to others and become a stepping stone to something greater. Surpassing all expectations, a growing community of enthusiasts find joy in «NAME»'s unique challenges and often learn something new.</p>

<h2 id="history">History</h2>
	<h3>Phase I</h3>
	<p>Many years elapsed since «NAME»'s first hit, the mini puzzle «A-UNLUCKY-UNLOCK», and the birth of the «SITE-NAME», just before 2018. Fast-forward a year, and five intriguing puzzles («A-WHIRLPUZZLE», «A-PMGRP», «A-HASTEFULLL», «A-TIARADVENTUR», «A-ABXTRACT-TRACTX»), came into being, each a stepping stone for the next project.</p>
	<p>«NAME»'s game-making adventure reaches a major milestone in 2019: «NAME»'s first and probably puzzlescript's first fully commercial game. Enter the «A-GRAVIRINTH» - a mysteriously puzzling exoarcheology expedition (now available for free).</p>
	<h3>Phase II</h3>
	<p> As a «A-GAME-JOURNEY» came to a close, «NAME»'s goals widened. Mastering puzzle-making became secondary to helping the <a href="2019-01-31-selected-puzzlescript-games.html">puzzle-making community</a> by providing useful tools and «A-11-lessons». Thus was born «A-PLATFORMER-TEMPLATE», tripling as a game, game-design tutorial and testing ground for... the «A-GAME-BAR»!
	<p>The «A-GAME-BAR» emerged as a collaborative open-source effort to provide puzzlescript <em>developers</em> and <em>players</em> with a better experience, including useful new features such as a level selector, background music manager and a hint system. This being the current focus, interspersing  <b>game bar</b> upgrades with short occasional hits like «A-BUROKKU-KONEKUTA» may nevertheless prove irresistible...</p>
