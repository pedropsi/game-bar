«ABOUT-TEXT»
<p><b>«TITLE:Load»</b> is a simple mini-game, of the sliding block type.  
Moving the pieces to the right positions unlocks each next level.</p>
<p>There are four easier levels + three harder levels.</p>

«HOWTO-TEXT»
<p>Move and push blocks with the <b>arrow keys</b>.</p>
