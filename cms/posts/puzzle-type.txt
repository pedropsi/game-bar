«HOWTO-TEXT»
<p>The screen displays only one word. Your goal is to type this word exactly as shown. However, your keyboard is behaving cryptically...  and the word is also your only clue!</p>
<p>You may type any of the 26 uppercase letters of the latin alphabet, and restart by pressing <kbd>backspace</kbd> or <kbd>delete</kbd>. Other characters or spaces are excluded.</p>
<p>All puzzles can be solved with logic and creative thinking, and maybe some pencil and paper. Please take your time, quick reactions are unnecessary.</p>
