«ABOUT-TEXT»
«ONE-LINER»

<h3>Easy quest</h3>
<p>Pick each <b>tiara</b> and hand it to the <b>Princess</b> to fulfill the <b>easy quest</b>.</p>
<p>Estimated play time: <b>30 minutes</b>.</p>
<h3>Hard quest</h3>
<p>Gently pick all <b>tiaras</b> unbroken, thereby making the <b>Princess</b> happier, a <b>harder quest</b> for which <em>precise timing</em> may sometimes be required.</p>
<p>Estimated play time: <b>60 minutes</b>.</p>
<h3>Hidden challenge</h3>
<p>Obtaining the <b>secret item</b>, after solving the <b>hidden challenge</b>, will subtly alter the game ending.</p>


<h2>Game elements</h2>

<h3>Characters</h3>
<p>The <b>Hero</b> is <b>you</b>!
<p>Behind her docile appearance, the <b>Princess</b> is actually in charge. Except when <b>wolves</b> attack... or any other puzzling situation has to be solved.
<p><b>Wolves</b> <em>chase</em> the closest human they find, and are not friendly. They will even <em>throw</em> <b>obtainables</b> <em>astray</em></b> and when <em>cornered</em> will <em>push</em> <b>Objects</b>.

<h3>Obtainables</h3>
<p>The precious <b>tiara</b> is so fragile that it will <em>shatter</em> under too much weight.</p>
<p>The <b>sword</b> can be used to keep wolves away and to <em>slash</em> <b>crates</b>.</p>
<p>One <b>secret item</b> may be discovered.</p>


<h3>Objects</h3>
<p><b>Crates</b> can be <em>pushed</em>, are <em>flammable</em> and <em>float</em> on <b>water</b>.
<p><b>Boulders</b> can also be <em>pushed</em> but are neither <em>flammable</em> nor <em>float</em> on <b>water</b>.
<p><b>Trees</b> can't move but are <em>flammable</em>.
<p><b>Torches</b> will <em>ignite</em> <b>trees</b> when <em>pushed</em> towards them and won't <em>float</em> on <b>water</b>.</p>

«HOWTO-TEXT»
<p><em>Move</em> with the «ARROWKEYS».</p>
<p>Come towards the <b>Princess</b> to <em>talk</em> to her. She is a great storyteller and on occasion may provide useful <b>hints</b>. You may <em>give</em> her certain <b>obtainables</b>.</p>
<p><em>Use</em> the <b>sword</b>, once obtained, by pressing <kbd>X</kbd>. You'll <em>swipe</em> it instinctively when dangerously close to a <b>wolf</b>.</p>
«UNDO-RESTART-TEXT»
<p>The game <em>saves automatically</em> whenever the <b>Princess</b> <em>safely</em> enters an area containing a new <b>tiara</b>.</p>
