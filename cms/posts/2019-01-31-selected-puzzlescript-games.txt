<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>«ONE-LINER»</p>

<h2>Criteria</h2>
<p>The list focusses on games released within the years <em>2014-2019</em>, yet many during this period were overlooked. As long as they match the <b>criteria</b> below, suggestions are «CONTACT-TEXT-5». </p>
<p>Please be aware that there's a separate list for «A-GAMES», as well as games where «NAME» collaborated (e.g. «A-GOPHERS») or was indirectly involved (e.g. «A-ENIGMASH»).</p>

<h3>Inclusion criteria:</h3>
<p>To be included, a game must score very highly in at least one of these <b>inclusion criteria</b>:</p>
<ul>
	<li><b>Atmosphere:</b> pleasant colours, agreeable music, cool animations;</li>
	<li><b>Challenge:</b> challenging puzzles;</li>
	<li><b>Originality:</b> a distinct style, goal, mechanics or story;</li>
	<li><b>Effort:</b> intense detail, innovative features.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Exclusion criteria:</h3>
<p>Fatal is the failure to meet at least one the following <b>exclusion criteria</b>:</p>
<ul>
	<li><b>Completeness:</b> not unfinished, not unpolished;</li>
	<li><b>Intuition:</b> lack of awkward controls, lack of visual confusion;</li>
	<li><b>Kinder-friendly:</b> absence of violence and of profanity;</li>
	<li><b>Minimalism:</b> no repetitions, no padding, no unnecessary moves, no wasted time;</li>
	<li><b>Optimism:</b> no negative feelings, at the end of the game;</li>
</ul>

<p><b>NB:</b> To add a <em>level selector</em> and <em>fullscreen</em> option to any of these games on the fly, just use the «A-BOOKMARKLET»!</p>
