«HOWTO-TEXT»
<p>The goal is marked by an <em>X</em>.</p>
<p>Move your <em>avatar</em> by pressing «LEFTKEY» or «RIGHTKEY». You may also <em>push blocks</em>. <em>Gravity</em> pulls <em>all downwards</em>.</p>  
<p>Press «UPKEY» to rotate the puzzle <em>clockwise</em> and «DOWNKEY» to rotate it <em>counterclockwise</em>.</p>
<p>Press <kbd>X</kbd> to enter <em>Preview Mode</em>, in which you may rotate <em>clockwise</em> by pressing «RIGHTKEY» and <em>counterclockwise</em> by pressing «LEFTKEY». In <em>Preview Mode</em>, nothing falls until you confirm, by pressing <kbd>X</kbd> again.</p>
