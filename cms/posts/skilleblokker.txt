<h2>F.A.Q.</h2>

<h3>How is «TITLE-BOLD» related to Burokku Konekuta?</h3>
<p>«TITLE-BOLD» is the sequel and opposite twin of <a href="burokku-konekuta.html">Burokku Konekuta</a>, following similar level structures and design conventions, but with an inverted goal and some extra twists, mostly relating to <b>sliding on ice</b>!</p>

<h3>Why is «TITLE-BOLD» in norwegian?</h3>
<p>The norwegian culture is marked by a colder climate.</p>
