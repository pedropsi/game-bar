<h2>F.A.Q.</h2>
<h3>Is «TITLE-BOLD» a real game or just a game template?</h3>
<p>Both! Because there is no better way to teach that to put the principles in practice, immediately. Questions like: <em>Why was this level not introduced before?</em> or <em>What is new in this level?</em> or <em>Could any element be removed while preserving the desired effect?</em> should guide your attention while playing.</p>

<h3>Is «TITLE-BOLD» a meta-game?</h3>
<p>Many people would agree with that description.</p>

<h3>Does it mean it's easier than other games?</h3>
<p>Don't be fooled by the first few levels - «TITLE-BOLD» is built step by step.</p>
