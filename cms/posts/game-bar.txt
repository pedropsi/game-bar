<h2>How to add the «TITLE-BOLD» to your own game</h2>
<h3>Step 1: include the code</h3>
<p>Include the <b>javascript source</b> and the <b>stylesheet</b> in a regular Puzzlescript export or in a custom page:</p>
<ul>
	<li>The <b>stylesheet</b>: <a href="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pedropsi/puzzlescript-game-bar/master/GameBar/GameBar.css" «BLANK»>GameBar.css</a></li>

	<li>The <b>javascript source</b>: <a href="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pedropsi/puzzlescript-game-bar/master/GameBar/GameBar.js" «BLANK»>GameBar.js</a></li>
</ul>

<p>The <b>stylesheet</b> can be added to the «[<»head«>]» via «[<»link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="GameBar.css" media="screen"/ «>]». </p>
<p>The <b>javascript source</b> must be loaded <b>after</b> the game loads completely, because a few of the original game functions are overwritten (mostly because of the level selector). So you can add to the end of the «[<»body«>]» this «[<»script type="text/javascript" src="GameBar.js"></script «>]».</p>


<h3>Step 2: select your game (custom pages only)</h3>
<h4>In the <b>javascript source</b> </h4>
<p>Ensure the  <b>javascript source</b> can find your game, by editing the line (near the end):
«[»var gameSelector="#gameCanvas";«]».
The selector named «[»"#gameCanvas"«]» should match the ID of your game canvas.</p>
<h4>In the <b>stylesheet</b></h4>
<p>Likewise, ensure the  <b>stylesheet</b> can find your game in fullscreen, by editing the selector «[»#gameCanvas«]» (first line) </p>
<p>In case your game bar is misaligned with wih your game, please edit also the selector «[».gameContainer«]» to match the <b>parent container</b> (outside) of your game canvas.</p>

<h3>Step 3 (optional): adding music</h3>
<p>To control a music playlist via the game bar, each music track should be added to the page inside «[<»audio«>]» tags, placed anywhere. If no audio tags are found, when pressing the music button, the message "Error: can't find the Jukebox" will be displayed.</p>
<p> To reveal the music button (hidden by default), simply uncomment the line «[»//ButtonOnClickHTML("♫",'ToggleCurrentSong(this)'),«]» by deleting the «[»//«]».</p>

<h2>Customisation tips</h2>
<h3>Colours</h3>
<p>Edit the colours variables in the «[»:root«]» element of the <b>stylesheet</b>.</p>

<h3>Font</h3>
<p>Edit the font variable (right after the colours) in the <b>stylesheet</b>.</p>

<h3>Logo image beside the level selector</h3>
<h4>Easy way</h4>
<p>Add a logo image named «[»"logo.png"«]» to the «[»images«]» folder in the root directory.</p>
<h4>Hard way</h4> 
<p>Edit the «[»function OpenBalloon«]» in the <b>javascript source</b>, specifically by changing the path string «[»"images/logo.png"«]» to wherever your logo may be located.</p>

<h3>"Solved level" ★ symbol</h3>
<p>For now, Ctrl+F to search for «[»★«]», which can be replaced by other symbols. More customisation options are planned.</p>

<h2>FAQ</h2>
<h3>Can you use «TITLE-BOLD» for free?</h3>
<p>Yes, it's under a «LICENSE-MIT» license.</p>

<h3>How can the «TITLE-BOLD» be used in the itch.io platform?</h3>
<p>Please ensure that the «TITLE-BOLD» <b>sources</b> are loaded from inside the «[<»iframe«>]» containing the game.</p>

<h3>Are all «TITLE-BOLD»'s features available elsewhere?</h3>
<p>The fullscreen button is new. The undo/reset and music buttons are found in Puzzlescript's default mobile menu and ««P-PRE»HAZELDEN«P-POS»» has made a custom level selector. Nevertheless, «NAME» found it useful to have a single bar with all important features.</p>

<h3>What are the differences between the shared «TITLE-BOLD» and the one <b>live</b> at the «SITE-NAME»?</h3>
<p>The <b>live</b> «TITLE-BOLD» at the «SITE-NAME» is the cutting-edge, sometimes experimental version. As soon as the changes are consolidated, they will be added to the official «TITLE-BOLD» shared in this page. Any buttons that depend on the «SITE-NAME»'s infrastructure to work or that lack general interest won't be included into the official «TITLE-BOLD».</p>

<h3>Does the «TITLE-BOLD» level selector handle checkpoints?</h3>
<p>Only in case all checkpoints are concentrated in a single level (e.g. a open world map such as in <a href="tiaradventur.html">Tiaradventur</a>). Support for multiple levels with checkpoints is not planned, unless you <a href="https://github.com/pedropsi/puzzlescript-game-bar/issues">actually need it</a>.</p>

<h3>Why can't the music button control music added via the youtube keyword in the prelude?</h3>
<p>Because this keyword is dubious.</p>

<h3>How to extend the «TITLE-BOLD» with custom buttons?</h3>
<p>Search for the «[»function GameBar(targetIDsel)«]» and edit the «[» buttons «]» array inside that function. In particular, try uncommenting the commented lines and see it change.</p>

<h4>How about the "Credits" and "How-to-play" buttons?</h4>
<p>You can either make your own or add the default ones (by uncommenting the lines referred to in the previous question). In the latter case you'll also need to have two sections in your page explicitly labelled with ids: «[»Credits«]» and «[»How-to-play«]».</p>
