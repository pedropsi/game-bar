Description

Creative Archive is an archive of Pedro PSI's personal projects - notably Pedro PSI's puzzles - with a triple purpose: record Pedro PSI's learning journey, make it useful to others and become a stepping stone to something greater. Surpassing all expectations, a growing community of enthusiasts find joy in Pedro PSI's unique challenges and often learn something new.

History

Phase I

Many years elapsed since Pedro PSI's first hit, the mini puzzle Unlucky Unlock, and the birth of the Creative Archive, just before 2018. Fast-forward a year, and five intriguing puzzles (Whirlpuzzle, Play Mini Gemini Replay, Hastefulll, Tiaradventur, Abxtract Tractx), came into being, each a stepping stone for the next project.

Pedro PSI's game-making adventure reaches a major milestone in 2019: Pedro PSI's first and probably puzzlescript's first fully commercial game. Enter the Gravirinth - a mysteriously puzzling exoarcheology expedition (now available for free).

Phase II

As a 11-game-making journey came to a close, Pedro PSI's goals widened. Mastering puzzle-making became secondary to helping the puzzle-making community by providing useful tools and 11 lessons. Thus was born Platformer Template, tripling as a game, game-design tutorial and testing ground for... the Pedro PSI's game bar!

The Pedro PSI's game bar emerged as a collaborative open-source effort to provide puzzlescript developers and players with a better experience, including useful new features such as a level selector, background music manager and a hint system. This being the current focus, interspersing game bar upgrades with short occasional hits like Burokku Konekuta may nevertheless prove irresistible...
