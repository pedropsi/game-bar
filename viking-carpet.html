Viking Carpet - Gold over Red

Over a dark red background, golden patterns: diagonal lines of geometric cereal surrounding either an eight petal flower, or a diamond with four squares inside. The cereal lines would meet at a cross. This pattern was found on a viking carpet on display at the Norsk Folkemuseum in Oslo.
